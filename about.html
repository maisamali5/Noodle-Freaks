ABOUT US

How it all started??

It all started when we were in our 2nd year of university. We were given a project to make a website on any topic of our choice. We were a group of 3 people and we decided to make a website on Healthiest noodles. We were all very fond of homemade chicken noodle Soup and we thought it would be a great idea to make a website on very fresh noodles. We started working on the noodle recipes website and we were very excited to achieve what we had planned. We first tried some fresh noodle recipes at home so that we can give a unique taste to the noodle reccipes which make it different from others. Our homemade chicken noodle recipe which we tried were Ramen noodle, Tuna egg noodle and Misu noodles. The main secret of making very fresh noodles is choosing healthy noodles that are suitable for everyonr.
